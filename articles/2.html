    Normal Mass Schedule Resumes

    By pastoralcentre| 2021-08-29T20:03:18+00:00 August 29th, 2021|Categories: Announcements, Blog|

    As of September 1st, the normal Mass schedule resumes in Polish parishes.  For the English Mass, this means that starting Sunday, September 5,  Mass is at 16:00 on Sundays and Holy Days of Obligation  through the end of June.  Confessions will be one-half hour before Mass starting at 15:30.
