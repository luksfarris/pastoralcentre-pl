3PM: The Hour of Mercy Divine Mercy Sunday, April 11
  • View Larger Image
    • 
    • 
    All members of the Pastoral Centre Community are invited to come to church at 3PM on Sunday, April 11, to join the parish congregation in praying the final prayers of the Divine Mercy Novena.  We will have adoration of the Blessed Sacrament during this time.  Two of the five decades of the Chaplet of Divine Mercy will be prayed in English (the rest in Polish).
     
    Normally, we would have adoration of Jesus in the Blessed Sacrament quite briefly at the end of our English Mass on Divine Mercy Sunday, with some prayers connected to the Divine Mercy.
     
    This year, however, we have a greater opportunity: to worship Christ in the Eucharist while joining with others in public recitation of the Chaplet of Divine Mercy – the final prayer in our novena of Divine Mercy this year.
     
    Please come to church at 3PM (15:00) on April 11 to pray together for God’s mercy on us, and on the whole world.
     
    The English Mass will be at 4PM (16:00) as usual on April 11.
