All Parish Meetings Cancelled
  • View Larger Image
    • 
    • 

    Because of the increased risk of coronavirus infection now, the parish is cancelling all gatherings of parishioners apart from Masses.

    This means that our social hours and board-games events are cancelled until further notice.  Also, instruction for converts is cancelled until further notice.

    At this point, we do not know if the International Rosary for Peace in the World planned for 29 October will have to be cancelled.

    Please remember to keep up the effort to avoid contagion:

    • Wear masks at Mass and be sure to cover BOTH your nose and your mouth at all times apart from when you are reading at the microphone or receiving communion.
    • Sanitize your hands when you come into the church.  If you are receiving communion on your hands, it is advisable to have your own hand sanitizer and sanitize your hands again before eating the Host which has rested in  your hands and been touched by your fingers (which may have picked up a microbe since being in the church; the priest will disinfect his hands before giving communion). 
    • Keep social distance during Mass.  Try to keep one pew apart from the people ahead of you and behind you, or sit at the extreme ends of the pews so you have a couple of meters between you and the nearest person.  It is OK to move to another pew if someone sits too close to you for your comfort or sense of health and safety.
    • Spread out when approaching the priest for communion and when kneeling on the step for communion.
    •  If you are coughing or sneezing or have a fever, stay home until you are well and/or until you have had a test and you know you do not have coronavirus.
