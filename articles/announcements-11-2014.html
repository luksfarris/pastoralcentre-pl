May 11, the Fourth Sunday of Easter, is the World Day of Prayer for Vocations. The World Day of Prayer for Vocations is also known as Vocations Sunday or Good Shepherd Sunday and will be celebrated this year on the theme ‘Vocations: Witness to the Truth’.

If you or someone you know is thinking about a vocation to the priesthood or to religious life, our links page is a great place to start your search. Scroll down to ‘Discerning a Religious or Priestly Vocation’ and a number of links will open.

You can read Pope Francis’s remarks about the World Day of Prayer for Vocations on the Vatican website.

Please pray for vocations in the month of May.
