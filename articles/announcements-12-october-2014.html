1. This Sunday is the nearest Sunday to the anniversary of the election of John Paul II to the papacy on 16 October 1979 – called ‘Pope’s Day‘ in Poland. The Polish Bishops’ Conference has decided that this year, Pope’s Day will be celebrated on October 12th. The theme of Pope’s Day this year is ‘Be Saints.’ At all the Masses on Sunday, the collection will be for a ‘living memorial’ to St John Paul II, that is, to fund scholarships for students who cannot otherwise afford their studies. Please be generous!
