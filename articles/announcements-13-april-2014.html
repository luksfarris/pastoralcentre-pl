1. This week is Holy Week. It is the last chance to have confession before Easter. If you still have not had confession, you can try to make an appointment with an English-speaking priest during the week.

2. I encourage everyone to join us for the Easter Triduum services this week on Holy Thursday, Good Friday and Holy Saturday. On Thursday and Friday, the services start at 6PM. On Saturday it is at 9:30PM. Arrive early, since the church fills up quickly.

3. The Divine Mercy Novena begins on Good Friday and ends on Divine Mercy Sunday, April 27th. We have provided booklets with the novena at the entrance of the church.

Fr Krzysztof
