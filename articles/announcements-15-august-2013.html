Please have a look at a special page on our website connected to the “ONE OF US” initiative.  This is a grass-roots pro-life initiative aimed at protecting human life from its beginning at conception.

If you are a citizen of an EU country, you can sign the petition.  If you are not a citizen of an EU country, you can – and should – send the information to people in your e-mail address book and/or publicize it using Facebook or Twitter.

The page on our site has a sample letter you can send to your friends.  It includes all the links your friends might want to follow to read more or to sign the petition.
