2. In summer, our priests take holidays and there are fewer priests available in the parish than there are during the academic year. This applies to our English-speaking priests as well. In practical terms, it means that there might be some days in the week when there are no English-speaking priests present in the parish. Sunday Mass will always be offered, but often there will only be one English-speaking priest available on Sunday. This means that if you want confession during July and August, you should arrive at Mass 20-30 minutes early and remember that the priest needs to leave the confessional 5 minutes before Mass begins: most of the time it will not be possible to have one priest at the altar and another in the confessional during the English Mass. If you need confession or to speak to a priest during the week, contact the priests directly, but be aware that there may be a wait until a priest is available who speaks English.
