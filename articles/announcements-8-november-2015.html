Our organist, Krzysztof Sobczuk, would like to invite everyone to a concert on Saturday, November 11 after the 6PM evening Mass at the Carmelite parish of St Joseph at 1 Ołbińska Street in Wrocław.

If you cannot attend Mass before the concert, plan to arrive at the church at about 18:45.

Jakub Zawadzki, a student at Academy of Music, will play organ pieces by Bach, Mendelssohn and Dubois. After the concert, free-will donations will be accepted for the restoration of the church’s organ.

St Joseph’s Church is beautiful and especially well-suited to appreciating music. You can see a view of the exterior of the church to help you recognize it when you get there, and a panoramic view of the unique circular interior here. (Click on the circle marked ‘Wirtualny Spacer’ above the small photos.)

You can find the church online using Google Maps, and you can plan your journey on public transport through Jakdojade.
