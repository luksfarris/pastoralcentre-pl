Ash Wednesday and Lent 2020

    We begin Lent on Ash Wednesday, 26 February. There will be no English Mass on Ash Wednesday. You are welcome to attend Mass in Polish at St Charles parish at 6.30, 7.00, 8.00, 10.00, 12.00, 17.00, 18.00 and 19.00. Most churches in Wroclaw should have an expanded schedule of Masses on Ash Wednesday. Check the announcements outside your nearest Church for details.

    On Fridays in Lent, Stations of the Cross will be held in all Catholic churches. You are also encouraged to use your own devotional materials in your own language to walk the Stations of the Cross on a private visit to any open church on Fridays in Lent. In St Charles Parish, the Stations of the Cross will be held on Fridays at 11:15 (before noon Mass) and 17:15 (before the Mass at 18:00) for adults; at 16:30 for children, and at 19:00 for young adults.

    You can read about how Ash Wednesday is celebrated in Poland (imposition of ashes may be different from in your country) as well as a helpful article about your Lenten duties as a Catholic and helpful hints about observing Lent on our website.
