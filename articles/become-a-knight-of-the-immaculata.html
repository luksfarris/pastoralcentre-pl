Become a Knight of the Immaculata

    The Pastoral Centre invites you consecrate yourself to the Immaculate Mother on the Feast of the Immaculate Conception, 8 December, 2021.

    You can make an act of consecration in any convenient way or time: at home, in a church, as a family, as a group of friends – you can  even consecrate your business to the Immaculata!

    It is good to be in a state of grace (go to confession first), and then to consecrate yourself, say this prayer:

    O Immaculata, Queen of Heaven and earth, refuge of sinners and our most loving Mother, God has willed to entrust the entire order of mercy to you. I, (name), a repentant sinner, cast myself at your feet, humbly imploring you to take me with all that I am and have, wholly to yourself as your possession and property. Please make of me, of all my powers of soul and body, of my whole life, death and eternity, whatever most pleases you.
    If it pleases you, use all that I am and have without reserve, wholly to accomplish what was said of you: “She will crush your head,” and “You alone have destroyed all heresies in the whole world.” Let me be a fit instrument in your immaculate and merciful hands for introducing and increasing your glory to the maximum in all the many strayed and indifferent souls, and thus help extend as far as possible the blessed kingdom of the most Sacred Heart of Jesus. For wherever you enter you obtain the grace of conversion and growth in holiness, since it is through your hands that all graces come to us from the most Sacred Heart of Jesus.
    V. Allow me to praise you, O Sacred Virgin
    R. Give me strength against your enemies
    Amen

    When you consecrate yourself to the Immaculata, you automatically become a Knight of the Immaculata.  This a society created by St Maximilian Kolbe.

    In 1917, when he was a seminarian in Rome, future Saint Maximilian Kolbe – then a simple Franciscan friar – witnessed outrageous attacks by Freemasons on the Catholic Church, including satanic images posted on the walls of the Vatican with offensive slogans about the Pope and the promise that the devil would crush the Catholic Church.

    Fr Maximilian understood that the Freemasons, who sponsored the attacks on churches, the Vatican and the papacy, had a plan for destroying the Church. According to Fr Maximilian, they knew that ‘”Catholicism can be overcome not by logical arguments but by corrupted morals.” And so they overwhelm the souls of men with the kind of literature and arts that will most easily destroy a sense of chaste morals, and foster sordid lifestyles in all phases of human life….’ In response to direct attacks on the Church and the corruption of moral life in general, Fr Maximilian created the Militia Immaculatae in Rome in 1917. 

    It has been said that Satan fears one person more than he fears God.  And that one person is the Blessed Virgin Mary.  Satan knows that he can never destroy God, so he attacks those who love God.  And Mary is the only perfectly pure, holy and sinless creature God created.  She is the one creature who belonged entirely to God; was completely open and obedient to God.  When Adam and Eve sinned, God promised that one day, one of Eve’s descendants would crush the head of the serpent who tempted Eve.  The Blessed Virgin Mary, by her perfect obedience, love and purity, cooperated with God’s plan.  She said, ‘Yes,’ to becoming the Mother of God.  Because of Mary’s ‘yes,’ Christ was born, preached, established his Church, and opened the way to heaven that had been closed since the fall of Adam and Eve.

    For this reason, Mary is the greatest enemy of Satan.  She is the one person he fears and hates more than any other, because through Mary’s ‘yes,’ Satan was defeated and heaven was opened to human souls again.

    Eve sinned and opened the way to sin for all of us.  Mary is the ‘new Eve’ who through obedience opens the way to heaven for all of us.  Dwight Campbell writes that

    ‘In God’s almighty providence, it is no mere coincidence that Kolbe founded the MI in the same year that Our Lady appeared to the three little children, Lucia, Jacinta and Francisco, at Fatima, Portugal and revealed to them God’s plan for world peace. “She will crush your head” should be read alongside Mary’s own words at Fatima: “In the end, my Immaculate Heart will triumph”; for the ultimate triumph of Mary’s Immaculate Heart involves the victory of the Immaculata — the All-Pure and Spotless Virgin whom the Evil One could never touch — over Satan and his minions.

    Today, we are seeing more and more open attacks on morality and the Church.  Most recently, women and students have been posting scandalous pictures on churches, disrupting Masses and tying up traffic in cities with ‘demonstrations’ in favor of killing unborn children, especially those who have the most need of our compassion and care, the handicapped. They are open and even obscene in their attacks on the Church – just as they were in 1917 in Rome.  Behavior that was illegal and unthinkable 50 years ago is now celebrated with parades and taught to small children as ‘lifestyle choices’ with no concern for children’s innocence and purity. 

    The Militia Immaculatae as its name indicates, is a ‘militia;’ an army or fighting force of Marian ‘knights.’ They don’t fight with the fascist strong-arm tactics of the minorities who shout slogans, spray-paint symbols on churches or attempt to shut down the normal activities of ordinary citizens by blocking traffic with demonstrations of their power. 

    Mary’s Knights seek first of all to model themselves on the humility, obedience, and love of the Blessed Virgin Mary.  They want to belong to Mary as their Mother, model and Queen.  By their prayers, sacrifices, penances and the holiness of their lives, members of the Miltia Immaculatae “seek to become ever more truly the property of the Immaculata; to belong to her in an ever more perfect way and under every aspect without any exception. They wish to develop their understanding of what it means to belong to her so that they may enlighten, reinvigorate, and set on fire the souls living in their own environment, and make them similar to themselves. They desire to conquer these souls for the Immaculata, so that in their turn they may belong without reserve, and may in this manner win an ever greater number of souls, to her — may win the entire world, in fact, and do so in the shortest possible time.” (Fr. Luigi Faccenda, O.F.M. Conv., One More Gift: Total Consecration to the Immaculata According to the Spirituality of Saint Maximilian Kolbe, pp. 51-52).
