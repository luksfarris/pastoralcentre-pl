Is it the truth that the Truth was born?
And that the Creator is becoming a creation?
Is there anything more beautiful?
Or is there another power that can glory in such a triumph?
Or is there, among all loving hearts, a Love purer and more exalted than this Love?

— Cyprian Norwid (Free translation by M. Brzyska)

Christ, the Light of the World

May Christ who found His dwelling-place in Mary’s womb also find your heart open to listen to Him – the Divine Child and the Eternal Logos.

May this listening engender in you new life, bringing to your hearts joy, peace and happiness, which can only find its fullness in Him.

We wish you a Merry Christmas.

Fr. Krzysztof Kukułka
Fr. Andrzej Kulczycki
