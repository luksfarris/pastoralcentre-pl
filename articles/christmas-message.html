The Nativity of Our Lord Jesus Christ

In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.

He was in the beginning with God.

All things came to be through him, and without him nothing came to be.

What came to be through him was life, and this life was the light of the human race;

the light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it.

John 1:1-5

Dear Brothers and Sisters,

As you celebrate Christmas

please consider that He Who became visible

is still invisible;

He Who became comprehensible

is always incomprehensible;

He Who became limited as Man

is eternally unlimited;

He Who is capable of suffering

is also incapable of suffering;

He Who can be expressed in words

is completely inexpressible.

May the Word who became man bring you joy, peace and love.

Along with these greetings we promise our prayers for you before the altar.

                                            The priests of the Pastoral Centre,

                                                                                        Fr. Krzysztof & Fr. Andrzej
