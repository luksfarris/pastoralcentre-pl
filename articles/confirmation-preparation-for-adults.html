Confirmation Preparation for Adults
  • View Larger Image

    The Pastoral Centre is organizing a course for adult English-speakers who have not yet had the Sacrament of Confirmation. The course will be held during the week, in the evenings, for the convenience of working people. It will be conducted in English by a native-speaker.

    The course should last 12-15 weeks, depending on the pace of the group, breaks for holidays, etc. We will meet in the parish rooms at ul. Krucza 58.

    When you have completed the course, the Pastoral Centre can help you with the process of being confirmed either in Poland or in your home country, by providing you with the necessary documentation that you have been prepared for the sacrament.

    If you need the Sacrament of Confirmation, contact us or come to Mass on Sunday at 4PM and talk to Fr Arek after Mass (just go into the sacristy at the end of Mass to talk to him).
