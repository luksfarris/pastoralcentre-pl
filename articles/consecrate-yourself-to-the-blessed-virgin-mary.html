    The Pastoral Centre for English-Speakers in Wrocław would like to invite baptized (and, ideally, confirmed) Catholics to consecrate themselves to the Mary, the Immaculate Conception, following the model of St Maximilian Maria Kolbe. You can become a Knight of the Immaculata by a simple act of consecration. We plan to do this together during Mass in our church at 7:30PM on Thursday, February 11, 2021, the Feast of Our Lady of Lourdes.
     
    If you would like to take part in the nine-day novena (February 2-10) concluding with Mass and Consecration, go to the sign-up form here.

    Booklets for the nine-day novena will be available (for free!) at the English Mass on the chair or table where you find the Lord’s Day bulletin with the Mass readings.  You can also access the same texts and prayers online in PDF.

    Fr Marcin will lead the  nine-day novena via ZOOM, from February 2 to February 10 at 8PM, so everyone can pray together online.
