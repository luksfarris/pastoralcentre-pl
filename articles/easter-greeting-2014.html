The Resurrection of Christ by Fra Angelico

May the Resurrected Lord
“who manifests himself
to the senses of our flesh
then of the mind”
through sight,
conversation
and interior calling
touch your heart
and be your peace
on the ways
which lead to Him.

With joyful greetings,

Fr Krzysztof Kukułka
Fr Andrzej Kulczycki
Fr Stanisław Rochowiak
