Easter Greeting 2015

Resurrection of Christ, Fra Angelico

On the occasion of the Resurrection of our Lord Jesus Christ,
we wish you and all those nearest to your hearts,
a healthy, happy, cheerful Easter, full of warmth, kindness and love.

We wish that this time will be an encounter with the Risen Jesus,
such as was experienced by his disciples and the women who went to the empty tomb.

May Jesus resurrect again in our hearts what has died away;
may he give life to the hopes that we have lost somewhere along the paths of our daily lives.

He alone is our Lord. He alone is our Way, Truth and Life.

— Fr Stanisław Rochowiak and Fr Andrzej Kulczycki
