THE PASTORAL CENTRE FOR ENGLISH SPEAKERS
AT THE ROMAN CATHOLIC PARISH OF SAINT CHARLES BORROMEO
58 KRUCZA STREET, WROCŁAW

According to the Code of Canon Law, a diocesan bishop is obliged to look after the entire congregation entrusted to his care, including both permanent and temporary residents, irrespective of their age, social status or nationality.

Acting in this spirit and in cooperation with Father Kazimierz Malinowski OFM Conv., the Provincial of the Franciscan Order of the Province of St Anthony and blessed Jakub Strzemię, in accordance with canon 383 § 1 of the Code of Canon Law, I do hereby create by this decree the Pastoral Centre for English Speakers at the Roman Catholic Parish of Saint Charles Boromeo on April 1st, 2007 at 58 Krucza Street, Wrocław.

I grant administration of the Centre to its Rector, appointed by the Diocesan Bishop of the Wrocław Archdiocese.

+ Marian Gołębiewski
Metropolitan Archbishop of Wrocław
