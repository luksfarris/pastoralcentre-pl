    It’s now possible to have childbirth classes in English in Wroclaw! OKSYTOCYNA provides doula, midwife, and physiotherapy services, and prenatal and postnatal care and support, in English.

    If you’re planning to have a baby in Poland, and need expert help in English, check out the services of Oksytocyna (Oxytocin, in English).

    English-language childbirth classes start in September.

    They also offer two different levels of home support after you have your baby, including the services of an English-speaking doula, a midwife and a physiotherapist. 

    Included among their services are

    • postpartum emotional support
    • My birth story – managing birth events and emotions
    • bonding support
    • getting a good start in breastfeeding
    • relaxing Rebozo massage
    • assistance in meal arrangement and family support planning
    • help in building your own supporting mother village
    • support in arranging your new family life with a baby (sleeping, time for yourself, duty sharing, mutual understanding and good family communication)
    • medical assessment – pregnancy, delivery and early postpartum
    • examination of the child
    • examination of the woman
    • breastfeeding assessment
    • help with lactation problems
    • assessment of wound healing in women, and if necessary, removal of stitches
    • how to take care of the pelvic floor muscles after delivery
    • learning how to care for umbilical cord, nails, skin
    • correct ways to lift and carry the child

    This is a great service for non-Polish-speaking women who want support and more control over their experience being pregnant, giving birth and adjusting to life with a new baby.  Some of the services would also make a great gift for a couple who are expecting a baby – especially if they don’t have family support while living in Poland.
