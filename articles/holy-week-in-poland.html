    You can read all about Holy Week in Poland on our website.  Our article will tell you what you need to know about

    • The Easter Triduum (Holy Thursday, Good Friday, Holy Saturday).
    • Blessing of Easter baskets on Holy Saturday.
    • The Divine Mercy Chaplet and indulgence.

    It’s especially useful to read through the pages about Holy Thursday, Good Friday and Holy Saturday if you want to attend liturgies in the church on those days, or if you want to bring an Easter basket to Church to be blessed on Holy Saturday.

    Blessing of Easter Baskets on Holy Saturday will be done between 10AM and 4PM, outside the church, standing on the grass near the large cross on the south side of the church (chapel entrance, Krucza street).
