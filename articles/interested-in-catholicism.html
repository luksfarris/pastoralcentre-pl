    The Pastoral Centre is currently planning to have a course for people who are interested in finding out more about the Catholic Faith or converting to Catholicism. If you are an English-speaker (non-Polish-speaker) living in the Wroclaw region, and interested in the Catholic faith for whatever reason, this course is intended for you.

    The course is only in the planning stage. In general, however, we think that it will begin in September and lead to reception into the Catholic Church within 18-20 months, depending on the progress of the participants. 

    If you have questions about the Catholic faith, you are welcome to attend the meetings. No one who comes will be obliged to finish the course or be pressured to convert. In the first several weeks, the course is concerned with presenting the basics of Catholicism and answering your immediate questions. Those who decide to continue learning will commit to coming to weekly meetings, since if they decide to enter the Catholic Church, every meeting will be necessary for their full instruction.

    There’s no obligation and nothing to fear by just coming and inquiring about the Catholic faith. If you think you might be interested in the course, contact us and we’ll put you on a list of people who have expressed interest. You’ll then be contacted in late summer to let you know full details about the course.

    If you are not yet at the point of making contact by email, you are welcome to attend Mass (6PM in July and August; 4PM the rest of the year) just to see what we do and perhaps approach someone for more information that way (please respect our faith in your mode of dress and behavior, as well as by refraining from going forward for communion).
