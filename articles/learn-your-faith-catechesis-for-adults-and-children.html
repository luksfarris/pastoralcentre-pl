    The Pastoral Centre is starting new lessons for the 2020-2021 academic year, with first meetings at the beginning of October.

    Fr Marcin Buntow is ready to begin preparing 9-year-0lds for their First Holy Communion next May.  If your child is – or will be – 9 years old by May 2021, and has not had First Holy Communion, please contact Fr Marcin directly.  You can talk to him after Mass (preferably before the first Sunday in October) or call him  on +48 791 776 446 or email him at mitja41@gmail.com.  Fr Marcin can also help you if your  English-speaking child is older than 9 years and still has not had First Holy Communion.

    At the beginning of October we will start lessons with Christians who wish to convert to the Catholic Church as well as non-Christians who wish to become Catholic.  If you are interested in this kind of instruction, contact us through the website contact page.    Please be sure to include a few details about yourself (country of origin and native language; your religious background or the extent of your religious education as a Catholic or other Christian; how long you will be in Poland; what has prompted your interest in the Catholic Church).  These lessons are also possible for adult, English-speaking Catholics who have been baptized but did not receive the sacraments of First Holy Communion and/or Confirmation. 

    In February and March 2021, we hope to have our annual Marriage Preparation Course.  If you are moving toward the sacrament of marriage, please read the information on our website concerning the steps you need to take before you consider setting a wedding date.  If you need marriage preparation in English, contact us and we’ll put you on the list of participants for the course next spring and notify you closer to the time when the course will begin.  We need at least 5 couples to sign up if we are going to conduct the course. 

    Please be aware that for a Catholic to enter into the Sacrament of Marriage, s/he must have completed the sacraments of initiation: Baptism, First Communion and Confirmation.  The Sacrament of Reconciliation is required before entering into the Sacrament of Matrimony as well.

    If you are a Catholic who has been away from the practice of your faith, and you would like to ‘get right with God’ and come home to the Catholic Church, we welcome you.  Please contact Fr Marcin for an appointment to talk, to prepare for confession, or to organize adult instruction complete the sacraments of initiation to help you learn the depth and beauty of your Catholic Faith, and help you learn how to practice your faith as an adult.
