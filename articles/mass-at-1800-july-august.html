Mass at 18:00: July & August
  • View Larger Image
    • 
    • 

    Starting July 2nd, the English Mass will be at 6PM on all the Sundays of July and August.  Mass will also be at 6PM on August 15th, the Feast of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary into heaven, which is a holy day of obligation in Poland.

    Please remember the time change, and don’t miss Mass in English in July and August.

    There will not be any Mass at 4PM or 5PM during summer, so if you come too early… you’ll have a long wait.  The last Mass (in Polish) will be at 7PM in July and August.
