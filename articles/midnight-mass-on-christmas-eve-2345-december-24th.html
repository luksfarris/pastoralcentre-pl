Midnight Mass on Christmas Eve, 23:45, December 24th
  • View Larger Image
    • 
    • 

    The Pastoral Centre invites you to attend Midnight Mass on Christmas Eve, December 24th, at the Ursuline Convent Chapel, Plac Nankiera 16, Wroclaw.

    We will gather for Mass in the convent at 23:45; the doors to the convent will be closed and Mass will begin at midnight.  Please arrive early in order to be sure that the door will still be open when you come.

    Due to covid restrictions, the chapel can only be occupied at 50% of capacity.  Therefore, we need you to sign up so we can be sure that we can seat all the people who would like to attend. (Even if you signed up before, expressing interest in attending the Midnight Mass, we need you to follow a new link and indicate that you definitely plan to come.) 

    The usual covid restrictions of wearing masks and keeping social distance apply.
