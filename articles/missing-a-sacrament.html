Missing a Sacrament?

    If you are a baptized Catholic adult, and you missed your First Communion and/or Confirmation, we can help you. The Pastoral Centre is planning a course to prepare non-Polish-speaking adults who are fluent in English for the Sacrament of Confirmation. We can also prepare people for First Communion at the same time.

    The course is only in the planning stage, but depending on the amount of interest and how long the course will take, we will start the course in September or October. We may also be able to repeat the course in late spring (after Easter) 2020, but again, that is only an idea at this time.

    Any English-speaking adult Catholic who has missed First Communion or Confirmation is eligible to attend the course (it will be necessary to commit to attending all the lessons).

    Once you complete the course, you will be able to have your missing sacraments through the Pastoral Centre (at one of our Masses, to be arranged by the Rector of the Pastoral Centre), through the cathedral in Wrocław, or in your own home country.

    Completing the Sacraments of Initiation (Baptism, First Communion, Confirmation) is necessary before you can enter into the married state. So people who are planning to marry in the Church need to make a special effort to complete these sacraments in advance of preparing for marriage. Ideally, you should try to be fully initiated into the Church before you approach a priest asking for the sacrament of Matrimony – it will make your entire marriage preparation process very much easier and smoother.

    If you need First Communion and/or Confirmation, contact us and we’ll put you on the list of interested people to be contacted when the course is organized. If you intend to attend the course, it will be helpful to have a copy of your Baptism certificate with you here in Poland (as well as proof of First Communion, if you had that sacrament) at the start of the course so we can verify which sacraments you are lacking.
