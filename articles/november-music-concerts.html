    To celebrate the installation of a state-of-the-art organ console at St Joseph’s Church in Wrocław, our organist invites everyone to a series of concerts in his parish church.

    Location: Parish Church under the patronage of St Joseph, ul Ołbińska 1.

    Admission: Free

    10 November, Friday after the 6PM Mass
    Organ Concert: Bach and Hindemith pieces played by Maciej Maszkiewski (the main organist in Wrocław Cathedral) and Karolina Opałka.

    11 November, Saturday (Polish Independence Day) 11:30AM
    The choir of St Charles Borromeo Parish, conducted by parish organist Irmina Zakowicz, will give a concert of Polish patriotic songs

    16 November, Thursday (Solemnity of Our Lady of Mercy), during the 6PM Mass,
    the organ console will be blessed.
    After Mass, Arkadiusz Popławski, renowned organist from Frombork Cathedral, will play his own renditions of popular organ pieces and music from films.
