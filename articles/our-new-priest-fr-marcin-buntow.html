Our New Priest, Fr Marcin Buntow
  • View Larger Image
    • 
    • 

    In October, we bid farewell to Fr Marek Kowalcze, who was with us as the Rector of the Pastoral Centre from 2019 to 2020.  Fr Marek has been assigned to the Franciscan parish in Harmęże, near Auschwitz and Kraków.  Fr Marek will be working with Fr Arek Góra on building the Franciscans’ presence on the Internet.  So we can look forward to more from Fr Marek along the lines of his YouTube series on the Theology of the Body.

    Our new Rector of the Pastoral Centre is Fr Marcin Buntow. Fr Marcin has celebrated the English Mass for us in the past.

    Fr Marcin was born on June 22, 1984 in Belarus. He lived in Poland until 1992, when his family moved to Ukraine, but maintained strong ties with the Polish community there. From 2002-2007, Fr Marcin was a student at the Maria Skłodowska-Curie University in Lublin in the Faculty of Political Science, where he earned a master’s degree in International Relations. From 2006-2007 he did postgraduate studies at the Catholic University of Lublin in the basics of Ukrainian law.

    In 2007, Fr Marcin joined the Order of the Franciscans Minor, Conventual (Conventual Franciscans). He made his solemn vows on 26 September 2009 and studied Philosophy at the Franciscan Theological Seminary in Kraków from 2009-2011. Fr Marcin was ordained a priest on 31 May, 2015, in Kraków.

    From 2004 Fr Marcin was very active in Polish Scouting. As a seminarian and priest, he has retained his ties to the scouting movement offering spiritual direction to scouts. Most recently, he was assigned to travel throughout the Conventual Franciscans’ Kraków Province giving retreats for people who were discerning a religious or priestly vocation.

    Fr Marcin was expected to take over his duties from Fr Marek as soon as Fr Marek left for Harmęże. However, sadly, Fr Marcin contracted Covid-19 while carrying out some summer duties in eastern Poland, so it was only on September 6 that Fr Marcin was able to return to Wrocław and take up his duties with us.  Fr Marcin celebrated his first Mass as Rector of the Pastoral Centre on 6 September, with a special guest, Fr Arek Żelechowski, who was the Rector from 2016-2019.  Fr Arek will be in Wrocław through September, then he will return to his studies in Rome.
