During Lent, we’re planning an overnight retreat on the 22nd/23rd of February.  We will stay in the retreat house of the Missionaries of the Holy Family in Szczytna, about 100km from Wrocław (about 1.5 hours by bus).  We will leave on Friday afternoon and return late Saturday.

Cost is about 65 zloties for meals and accommodation (not including transportation).

This is a good opportunity to leave the city and spend some time in prayer, silence and adoration as preparation for Easter.  If you are interested in going on this retreat, contact Father Krzysztof .
