    Saint Charles (Sw Karol) parish live-stream for people who cannot attend Mass in person has been restored.  Here’s the link for the parish’s Youtube page:  Franciszkanie Boromeusz Wrocław.  If you encounter problems with the live transmission in future, go to the parish website and look for the ‘live transmission’ button under the main image at the top of the page (TRANSMISJE NA ŻYWO). If it says ‘czasowo zawieszone,’ it means that transmission is no longer working.  Otherwise, click on that button and it should take you to the live-feed.
