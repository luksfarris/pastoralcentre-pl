    European Taizé Meeting: Everyone is welcome – without tickets, without age limits..

    Evening prayer with thousands of young people from all over Europe will be held in Hala Stulecia, December 28-31 at 7pm.

    Hot tea with young people in the city centre takes place in Plac Wolności, December 30 and 31 at noon.

    You’re welcome to join in midday prayer in the churches of the city centre December 30 and 31 at 1pm:

    • Cathedral of St John the Baptist
    • Greek-Catholic Cathedral of Sts Vincent and James
    • Evangelical Church of the Divine Providence
    • Church of the Holy Cross
    • Our Lady of the Sands Church
    • Church of the Most Holy Name of Jesus
    • Dominican Church of St Wojciech (Adalbert)
    • Corpus Christi Church

    There will also be meetings and workshops held in various parish churches.  You can find a detailed programme in English online here.
