Thursday, 3 June: Corpus Christi, Holy Day of Obligation
  • View Larger Image
    • 
    • 

    On June 3 at 8PM in the Church of Saint Charles Borromeo, the Pastoral Centre will have its first-ever English Mass for the Solemnity of the Body and Blood of Christ (in Latin, Corpus Christi).  After Mass, we will have a Eucharistic procession around the church, outside.  Then we will return inside the church and have short adoration and Fr Marcin will bless us with the Most Holy Eucharist (benediction).

    June 3 is a national holiday, but also a Holy Day of Obligation.  It means that all Catholics are obliged to attend Holy Mass on June 3, the Solemnity of the Body and Blood of Christ.  If you are travelling, you need to find a Catholic Church and attend Mass on Thursday.   If you fail to attend Mass on a Holy Day of Obligation, it is a serious sin:  you must go to confession before you are able to receive Holy Communion again.

    We look forward to seeing you on Thursday, 3 June at 20:00 for the English Corpus Christi Mass!
